Orfeu

Orfeu /ɔːˈfeɪjuː/

Orfeu is a modern reimagining of the mythological figure Orpheus, inspired by the 1959 film "Orfeu Negro." The project aims to showcase the music of the world, connecting the ancient god of music with contemporary sounds and cultural expressions.

Example: "The Orfeu project offers a glimpse into the diverse musical landscapes of different cultures."
